About Me

"Hello, I'm Gaurav Gupta, a second-year B.Tech student specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, situated in the vibrant setting of Kattankulathur, Chengalpattu Dt., Tamil Nadu-603203. Born on the 3rd of September 2003 in Lucknow, I've completed my education, from class 10th to 12th, at Army Public School Nehru Road, Lucknow Cantt. Coming from a defense background, with my father serving in the Indian Navy and my grandfather in the Indian Army, a sense of discipline and commitment is deeply ingrained in me.In my family of four, I've inherited a love for diverse activities. My hobbies range from swimming, playing the guitar, horse riding, trekking, and going on camping expeditions, to the joy of cooking and the thrill of solving coding-related problems. Among my achievements, I take pride in being a three-time national level swimmer, a certified open water scuba diver, a state-level equestrian show jumper, and holding a purple belt in karate.

My journey in technology is defined by my adeptness in languages such as Python, Java, and C++, and my proficiency in problem-solving through algorithms and data structures. Moreover, my skills extend to crafting dynamic interfaces through web development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. However, my true passion lies in the immersive world of AI and ML, where I explore neural networks, deep learning, and comprehensive data analysis. Eager to contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of technology, I am driven by the endless possibilities and the potential to make impactful innovations in this dynamic field."
